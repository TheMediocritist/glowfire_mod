TerminusTTF contains the original bitmaps in addition to scalable outlines.
At their designed sizes, the bitmaps are very sharp.
Only certain sizes are bitmapped.

 These are the font sizes you should use (the point sizes assume that your display has a resolution of 96 dpi):

12 px or 9 pt
14 px or 10.5 pt
16 px or 12 pt
18 px or 13.5 pt
20 px or 15 pt
22 px or 16.5 pt
24 px or 18 pt
28 px or 21 pt
32 px or 24 pt
